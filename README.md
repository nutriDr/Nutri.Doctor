## Using the Search Engine

### How the search engine works?

Nutri Doctor lets you search for supplement products by their ingredients. You add the ingredients you want, optionally exclude ingredients you want to avoid, and the search engine returns products that match your criteria — across multiple merchants simultaneously.

Results are ranked to show you the fewest products needed to cover all the ingredients you're looking for, making it easy to find efficient, targeted combinations.

### How do I add an ingredient to my search?

Type the name of an ingredient into the search box. A dropdown will appear with matching ingredients from our database. Select an ingredient from the list to add it.

Once added, the ingredient appears as a tag with a green **[ + ]** symbol. Search results update automatically. You can add up to five ingredients at a time.

### How do I exclude an ingredient?

Click or tap an added ingredient tag once. The green **[ + ]** symbol changes to a dark red **[ − ]** and the label shows "Excluded." Results will automatically update to hide any products containing that ingredient.

Useful for allergies, sensitivities, or simply avoiding specific compounds.

### How do I remove an ingredient from my search?

Each ingredient tag cycles through three states when clicked:

| Click | Symbol | State      | What it does                              |
|-------|--------|------------|-------------------------------------------|
| 1st   | **−**  | Excluded   | Products with this ingredient are hidden  |
| 2nd   | **×**  | Delete     | Tap to confirm removal                    |
| 3rd   | **+**  | Added      | Ingredient is added back                  |

To clear your entire search at once, use the reset icon (three disks) at the bottom right of the search box.

### How do I choose which merchants to include?

Click the menu icon in the top right corner. Toggle merchants on or off. Active merchants appear in green, excluded ones in red. At least one merchant must remain selected.

### Why is there a five-ingredient limit?

The limit keeps results focused and manageable. If there is enough demand, this limit may be increased in the future.

## Data & Accuracy

### Where does the product data come from?

Product information (ingredients, amounts, descriptions, prices) is sourced directly from third-party merchant websites. Nutri Doctor does not manufacture, sell, or fulfill any products.

### How accurate and current is the data?

We update merchant data periodically, but formulations, availability, and prices can change. The data shown may not always reflect the most current information.

> **⚠️ Important**: Always verify ingredient information directly on the merchant's product page before purchasing — especially if you have allergies or specific health requirements.

## Health & Privacy

### Is this medical advice?

**No.** Nutri Doctor is a search and comparison tool for informational purposes only. Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendation.

**Always consult a qualified healthcare professional** before starting or changing any supplement regimen.

### What data does Nutri Doctor collect about me?

Nutri Doctor does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as name or email.

We use **Google Analytics** to collect anonymized usage data (pages visited, search activity, device type, etc.) to help improve the service. You can opt out using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
